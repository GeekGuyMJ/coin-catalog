Everything You Need

Built by a collector, for collectors.

6,800+ US Coins

Complete database from Half Cents to Modern Dollars. Key dates, proofs, errors, varieties — all pre-loaded.

Live Metal Prices

Gold, silver, platinum, palladium, copper, nickel — auto-updated every minute. Portfolio values recalculated instantly.

Bullion & More

Track raw bullion, scrap metal, paper currency, bulk coins by weight, and custom collectable categories — all in one portfolio.

Your Data, Your Way

Backup to WebDAV (Nextcloud, ownCloud) or download a JSON export. No accounts. No servers. You hold the keys.

Offline-First PWA

Works without internet. Install on your phone or desktop. Add coins on a plane — syncs when you're back online.

Wishlist & Goals

Heart any coin to add to wishlist. Set target grades and max prices. Mark acquired with one tap. Track completion by series.
