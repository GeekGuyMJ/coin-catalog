Privacy Policy

Effective: July 2026  |  Version: 1.0

Data We Collect

None. This app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers.

Local Storage Only

All your collection data (coins, bullion, notes, photos, settings) stays on your device in your browser's IndexedDB or the app's local database. We never see it.

Optional Cloud Backup

You may choose to export a backup file to your own cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, iCloud, local folder). This is initiated by you, stored in your account, and we have no access to it.

Network Requests

  • Live metal prices: Fetched from public APIs (no authentication, no user data sent).
  • Coin images: Loaded from our static CDN (no user identification).

Permissions

The Android app requests no special permissions (no camera, location, contacts, microphone, etc.).

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

Not directed at children under 13. No personal data is collected from anyone.

Data Retention

We retain no user data. Your local data remains until you delete the app or clear its storage.

Your Rights

Since we hold no data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Your data is entirely under your control.

Changes

Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
